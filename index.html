Future Marriage Agreement

I, Rafid, hereby declare that if I am blessed with a daughter, I will give her in marriage as a sadaqah to Shams. He will marry her regardless of her beauty, character, or any shortcomings, except if she ceases to be a Muslim. This declaration is made sincerely, acknowledging the seriousness of promises and their weight in the sight of Allah.

Terms & Conditions

  1. The Groom expresses his noble intention to propose marriage to the daughter of The Father once she reaches a suitable age (17–19 years).
  2. The Father acknowledges the Groom’s honorable character, Islamic values, and promising future.
  3. The Groom shall treat The Father to a feast upon signing of this agreement. (No biryani)
  4. The price of Mahr will be granted by Shams as per his noble character, and once decided, it cannot be renegotiated.
  5. Any disputes arising will be settled by an Exploding Kittens match under fair Islamic conduct.
